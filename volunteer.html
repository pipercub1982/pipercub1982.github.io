Volunteering

Your hospital needs your help!

The Rolling Plains Memorial Hospital Auxiliary would like to take this opportunity to invite you to volunteer with us. One of the most valuable resources at RPMH is the Auxiliary. We, as volunteers, feel we are individuals with a common goal – superior service. Service to our community, our hospital and our neighbors is very important, but we cannot do it alone.

The hours we volunteer mean a friendly smile and a comforting touch to so many in need, making Rolling Plains Memorial Hospital a better place. If you care about people, we need you as a volunteer. No experience is needed. Each new member receives a basic orientation to the hospital and to our responsibilities. Annual general training is also provided.

Interested?

If you are interested in becoming a hospital volunteer, contact our director of volunteer services at (325)235-1701 ext. 6222. You can also visit with any of our volunteers in the Outpatient Lobby or at the front lobby/gift shop area. We’ll be glad to answer any questions you may have.

You may also download our volunteer application and submit it to the hospital to join our important team.
