Rural Health Clinic

Schedule an appointment or use our "Walk-in Hours" to get the care you need.

201 East Arizona, Sweetwater, TX

The Rolling Plains Memorial Hospital Rural Health Clinic provides quality medical care for the whole family. This clinic allows the hospital to provide the community with excellent healthcare in a fast and caring manner. Our staff is here to help with everything from minor emergencies to more serious health concerns.

Walk-In Clinic

One of the primary functions of the Rural Health Clinic is to provide the citizens of Nolan County with a healthcare facility that does not require an appointment in advance.

If a member of your family develops a healthcare situation, our team of Nurse Practitioners and Medical Doctors are here to help during when your health problem arises.

Scheduled Appointments

To make coming to the Rural Health Clinic as easy as possible for our patients and their families, you can also schedule an appointment during normal business hours. Scheduling an appoingment allows patients to know when they will be seen without having to wait for the next available caregiver.

Illness and Injuries

The Rural Health Clinic can provide treatment for:

  • Cough and Colds
  • Flu & Virus
  • Flu Vaccines
  • Allergies
  • Fever
  • Ear, Throat and Sinus Infections
  • Urinary Tract Infections
  • Nausea, Vomiting, and Diarrhea
  • Bronchitis
  • Skin Infections
  • Eye Infections
  • Ear Cleaning
  • Burns, Cuts, Sprains
  • Earache or Infections
  • Sore Throat
  • Back Pain
  • Heartburn

Other Services

Outpatient Lab Services - Patients that need bloodwork can schedule an appointment and have that taken care of through the clinic.

Behavioral Health Services - We are proud to be albe to provide Behavior Health Services to Sweetwater and the Nolan County Surrouding area.
