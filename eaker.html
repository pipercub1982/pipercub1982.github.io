Eaker Family Medicine is happy to take care of multiple generational families. There is no better care than being cared for with yoyu family, by ou family. The Eaker Family Medicine staff has several generational family patients, including the Wilks/Sells family above.

Non-OB Pediatric Medicine

Caring for your little one is just one of the many ways that Eaker Family Medicine serves our community. Routine pediatric care, vaccinations, yearly physicals, sick visits, and much more are avilable.

Well-Woman Care

Eaker Family Medicine provides Well-Woman Care. Well-Women care is an important preventative praactice that is vital to a woman's health. It is important to get your well-woman annually, regardless of how healthy you feel you are. Annual well0woman care centers around annual screenings and tests as well as oerall health maintenance. Eaker Family Medicine cares fo rthe women in this community to promote women's health.

Sports Physicals

Eaker Family Medicine is your stop for your child's sports physical. A sports physical will cover all of the basics of your child's health. A sports physical or pre-participation exam (PPE) can help to indentify health problems that would hinder your child from playing safely.

Well-Child Care

Eaker Family Medicine gives the opportunity to ensure your child is protected from infections diseases by reviewing and updating theier immunizations with an annual well-child visit.

Cardiac Stress Testing

Eaker Family Medicine provides cardiac stress tests. These are a cardiological test that measures the heart's ability to respond to external stress in a controlled environment.

Allergy Testing

Get your allergy testing at Eaker Family Medicine. Allergies are a common occurance in West Texas and our office can help you identify wat allergies are affecting you.
