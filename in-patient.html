Inpatient Care

Our professional staff and 35-bed unit help keep our community cared for.

The Rolling Plains Memorial Hospital Inpatient Care services provides professional nursing staff and a team of ancillary technical staff in a 35-bed medical surgical unit.

Adult and pediatric patients who require post-operative care for major and minor surgery as well as diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic medical conditions will find a caring and compassionate team ready to meet their healthcare needs.

A fully equipped six bed Intensive Care Unit provides the specially trained staff with necessary data to monitor patients with critical medical and surgical problems.
