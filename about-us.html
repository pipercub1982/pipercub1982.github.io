About Us

Nolan County Hospital District under the business name Rolling Plains Memorial Hospital provides healthcare for the communities of Nolan County.

Founded in 1976, Rolling Plains Memorial Hospital offers a wealth of medical services including:

  • 24 hour emergency care
  • Obstetrics
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech Therapy
  • Sleep Studies
  • Advanced radiology services including CT scanning and MRIs
  • Outpatient lab
  • Physical Therapy
  • Sugical Services
  • Swing Bed Services
  • Patient Education
  • Home Health
  • And More
